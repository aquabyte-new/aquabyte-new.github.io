Princeton Kwong

Princeton was formerly the Software Engineering Manager at Memebox Corporation, a $160 million venture-backed Y-Combinator company and a global e-commerce, recommendation, and community platform for Korean cosmetics with operations in the US, Korea, and greater China. He specializes in AI recommendation and facial recognition, distributed web systems, native mobile development, and data pipelines.
